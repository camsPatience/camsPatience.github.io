Character Files

Hesse Vios

Hesse is a Keyblade-wielding boy hailing from the city of Greenfloe, formerly living with his cousin Tela. He is one of the three Keyblade wielders who landed in Traverse Town after their home worlds were destroyed simultaneously.

Combat

Hesse wields the Pain of Solitude, a Keyblade specializing in magical power. Consequently, the few spells he has in his arsenal are buffed. Currently, Hesse hasn't had the chance to develop a proper fighting style.
The spells he does know are as follows:
Reflect - A spell which briefly produces a barrier of light that blocks enemy attacks. Moments after Reflect blocks an attack, the barrier shatters into a series of light explosions around Hesse. The barrier also will react to self-inflicted physical contact with a target, meaning Hesse throwing himself at an enemy is a valid strategy.
Water - A spell which briefly surrounds Hesse in a water barrier to shield him from attacks, before launching a water projectile that homes in on a target. When used on a target that's beneath the surface, like a Shadow lurking in the ground, the target will be forcibly surfaced.

Story

Hesse used to live with his cousin Tela in their home city of Greenfloe, acting as what Tela dubbed "freelance heroes" (more accurately, vigilantes). This went fairly well for a while, until one fateful day...
Their mission went wrong. Horribly wrong. They were caught while sneaking around a certain "big boss's" tower, and got swarmed with enemies. Tela was enraged. Angrier than Hesse had ever seen her. And she was going berserk. At the rate she was fighting, everyone in the room, herself and Hesse included, would be dead before long. So, Hesse did the only thing he could think to do.
He dove at Tela.
It happened too fast. A flash of light. A pair of broken legs. A scared boy frantically trying to take his cousin home, trying to make himself small. And a word branded into the back of his mind.

Keyblade.

For nearly a year, things went on almost as normal, save for... Tela. Hesse was afraid to let himself near her, and she was always bitter with him. Hesse was forbidden from ever thinking of using the Keyblade, the weapon that had taken his cousin's legs.
Home life was tense. Hesse constantly found excuses to be away, to get out of their apartment, to not have to think about it all. When he was away, it all went away. Life outside was almost pleasant.

And then the final day dawned.

That morning was eerily quiet. The part of town where Hesse lived was usually quiet enough as-is, but something was different. It felt like something was looming.
This feeling continued throughout the entire day. The sun began to be shrouded by dark clouds. The light grew dim, and the shadows grew deeper. Stronger. That evening, that sinking feeling was given form.

The Heartless arrived.

Their world was connected.

Tied to the darkness.

Soon to be completely eclipsed.

Hesse screamed for his cousin,
and blacked out.
